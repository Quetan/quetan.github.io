Template Installation

  1. FTP Upload:
    • Open up your FTP manager and connect to your hosting
    • Browse to required directory (Normally public_html)
    • Choose the Demo you want to use from MASON folder for your website
    • Upload the css, js, images, favicon, fonts files, contact.php and the particular demo (from the demos) of your choice from MASON folder.

Pages

    • index-particles.html is for particles effect home page
